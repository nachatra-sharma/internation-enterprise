About Us: International Enterprises

Welcome to International Enterprises, where integrity meets innovation to seamlessly connect global markets through efficient import-export solutions. Established in 1988, we've been at the forefront of international trade, serving as a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable and tailored trade and logisitics solutions.

Mission Statement:

At International Enterprises, our mission is clear - to foster international trade relationships by delivering excellence. We are dedicated to ensuring the timely and secure movement of goods across borders, driven by our commitment to integrity, innovation, and customer-centricity.

Values That Drive Us:

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and adaptability.
  • Customer-Centric: Putting our clients' needs at the forefront.
  • Teamwork: Collaborating for collective success.
  • Quality: Ensuring excellence in every aspect.
  • Adaptability: Navigating the complexities of international trade.

Products and Services:

International Enterprises specializes in the import and export of a diverse range of products, including mobile phones, home appliances, textiles & garments, calculators, confectionery, and sundry items. Our unique selling proposition lies in our exceptional sourcing capabilities and deep understanding of customer needs.

Company History:

Founded in 1988, we started as a leading exporter of photographic goods with exclusive distributorship of renowned brands such as Yashica, Vivitar, and Leica. Over the years, we expanded into various categories, achieving notable milestones, including the highest market share in the Asia-Oceania region for Panasonic batteries.

Vision for the Future:

As we envision the future, International Enterprises is committed to becoming the foremost global facilitator of trade, setting the standard for excellence in import-export services. We see a dynamic and interconnected world where businesses seamlessly collaborate across borders, and we are determined to be the driving force behind this transformative change.

At International Enterprises, we don't just navigate the complexities of international trade; we redefine them. Join us on the journey to a future where trade knows no borders.
